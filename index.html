Collection Concept
Love Cycle No.1: born and spoil with family love
Love Cycle No.2: exploring adolescent with frienship love
Love Cycle No.3: met someone special with romantic love
Love Cycle No.4: Wisdom of life, key to self love

This collection conveys a cycle of four kinds of love most of us encounter in life.
Through the discovery process, we receive unconditional love from family at the first stage, and as we reach to a certain age,
we learn to give love for others like our friends and our other half.
Once we have experienced different stages of love, we went through ups and downs, and the last step is to learn from everything we have come across in life
to fully understand only by loving ourselves is the key to embrace our true identity and to bring positivity into the world.
